Mini-workshop on "Integrable Deformations of CFTs"

Date: November 28 (Wednesday), 2018

Venue: Conference Room 322, Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences

Introduction

Conformal field theories and holographic dualities play important roles in modern theoretical physics.
This mini-workshop will be focusing on recent developments on integrable deformations of conformal
field theories and their holographic duals.

Invited speakers:

Luis Apolo (YMSC-Tsinghua), Soumangsu Chakraborty (Hebrew U.), Bin Chen (Peking U.), Junbao Wu (Tianjin U.)
